Privacy Policy — 500 Score Keeper

Last updated: 2026-07-08

500 Score Keeper does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no server backend and makes no network requests.

Data storage

All game data — team names, scores, and history — is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Permissions

The Android app requests the INTERNET permission only to load the app shell. No data is sent or received over the network.

Third parties

No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs are used.
